National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC)

NIT Calicut, located in Kozhikode, Kerala, is one of India's leading technical institutions. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, architecture, science, and management. Known for its academic excellence and beautiful campus nestled in the Western Ghats, NITC promotes innovation and holistic development.
