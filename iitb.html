Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay)

IIT Bombay, located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, is one of India’s premier engineering institutions. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across diverse engineering, science, and management disciplines. Renowned for its research output and vibrant campus life, IIT Bombay stands as a beacon of academic excellence and innovation.
